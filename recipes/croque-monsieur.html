Croque Monsieur

Description

Croque Monsieur is a delicious French ham and cheese sandwich made with Gruyère, ham and a simple béchamel sauce

Ingredients

  • Béchamel sauce
    • 25g butter
    • 15g flour
    • 250ml whole milk
    • salt and black pepper
  • Slices of white bread
  • Slices of ham
  • Slices of Gruyère cheese

Steps

  1. Béchamel sauce
    1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in flour and cook, stirring constantly, for about 3 minutes.
    2. Gradually add milk, stirring well until the mixture is smooth. Cook, stirring, until sauce is thickened.
    3. Season with a little bit of salt and pepper
  2. Cover each bread slice with a layer of béchamel, spreading it all the way to the edges
  3. Top each slice with a piece of ham and Gruyère cheese.
  4. Bake at 200°C for about 7-8 minutes, until the cheese is melted
