Salmon Pasta

Description

A creamy garlic sauce with pasta of your choice is quick (under 30 minutes!) and easy to make. Get ready to impress your friends and family, and yourself!

Ingredients

  • 300g salmon fillets
  • 500g your pasta of choice
  • 400ml heavy cream
  • fresh lemon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ onion
  • salt and pepper
  • olive oil
  • butter

Steps

  1. Cook your pasta of choice to al dente in a large salted pot following package instructions.
  2. Meanwhile, add olive oil and cook the salmon for a couple of minutes on both sides, then set it aside for later.
  3. Chop ½ an onion into fine pieces and put it in the pan along with a bit of butter for about a minute.
  4. Add minced garlic and the cream along with some fresh lemon juice. Continue with adding salt and pepper with your own discretion.
  5. Get your side-lined salmon back from the bench and add it on the pan. Let simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Drain the pasta and mix it to the pan. Food is ready to be served immediately.
